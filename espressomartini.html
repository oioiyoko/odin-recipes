Espresso Martini

My favorite cocktail.

The classic espresso martini, also known as a vodka espresso, is a cold caffeinated alcoholic drink made with espresso, coffee liqueur, and vodka. It is not a true martini as it contains neither gin nor vermouth, but is one of many drinks that incorporate the term martini into their names.

  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with 3 coffee beans.
